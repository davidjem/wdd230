Bountiful Foods

Family operated since 1998

Our business is ready to serve you and your family. We are pioneers in the region. Because we control the entire process from seed to store, Bountiful Foods use zero pesticides, 95% less water, and are 100+ times more productive on the same footprint of land than traditional agriculture. Our produce is currently available at select Whole Foods and Foragers stores in the California area and featured on the menus of local restaurants.

The company is driven by a mission to make nutritious food more accessible. Its products are now available in 800 stores, including Target, Kroger, and Whole Foods.

Each of these services is is customized to meet the needs of the client. The company’s mission is to empower people to live healthy and blissful lives.
